Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. Calculate your BMI to determine if you're in a healthy weight range.

BMI Categories:

Underweight: < 18.5
Normal weight: 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight: 25 - 29.9
Obese: ≥ 30
